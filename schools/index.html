Mirobot is made to teach

Mirobot is unique in how it takes a cross-curricular approach to learning. By tying together different subject areas, childrens' learning is strengthened as they apply learning from one subject area to another.

Design & Technology

Learn about the mechanics that make Mirobot move and how everything fits together. Tie in to robotics in the curriculum and learn what makes it move.

Computing

Use the range of existing apps to teach children about the basic principles of computing. Start with a simple visual programming tool and work up to using real languages like Javascript.

Mathematics

Using turtle robotics to draw geometric shapes is a classic way of getting children engaged in thinking about physical mathematical problems.
