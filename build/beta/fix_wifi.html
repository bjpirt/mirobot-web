Instructions

This should enable Mirobot to get on your WiFi network. Follow these instructions:

  • Download: https://github.com/bjpirt/mirobot-ui/releases/download/201408141112/mirobot.bin
  • Turn on Mirobot and join its network
  • Go to http://10.10.100.254/iweb.html
  • In the second part of the page that says "Upgrade customized webpage" select the mirobot.bin file you just downloaded and click "Upload" (If on a Mac, use Safari)
  • Wait for it to say it worked then wait another 20 seconds
  • Visit http://10.10.100.254/ and try to configure the WiFi again
